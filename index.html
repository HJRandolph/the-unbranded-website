SARAH BELING

Founder Advisor

Copywriting

Enamored at a young age with storytelling, Sarah tortured her family and friends throughout the 90s with re-worked revivals of classics like The Wizard of Oz and Annie (in which she gracefully accepted the title role). Sarah eventually moved to New York, attended NYU Tisch to study acting, threw out all of her clothing that wasn’t black, and developed a taste for bodega breakfast sandwiches. Sarah writes plays that range from thriller to 80s musical parody and is eager to help others tell their stories.

EMILY BRADSHAW

Video Editing

Emily is a video editor based out of New York City. She uses her BA in Cinema Studies from New York University as well as her undying love of film to educate her choices as editor. She is also an actress and improviser.

Jennifer Cameron

Web Development

A reluctant "techy," Jennifer started life with a strong emphasis on the arts: painting, acting, dance, and drawing (including an unfortunate incident involving a sharpie and her parent's carpet). However, a strong desire and affinity for logic and tech activities flowed though all aspects of life, school and work until she could no longer deny the pull and jumped into the world of web development. Jennifer now focuses on feeding both parts of her soul by creating things that are pleasing and functional.

AMELITA LIJEK

Founder

Business Ops and Design

Amelita has always had an instinct for management and organization. Beginning her executive career at age four when she dutifully (and, in retrospect, likely annoyingly) pointed out who was off of their line in ballet class, she has honed that intuition through a wide variety of jobs – working in everything from retail and fine dining to musical comedy and film production, with pit stops along the way to pick up some dog training skills and an undergrad degree from NYU. Her non-traditional path (though stressful for her parents) has taught her that following her gut when it comes to ethical values is essential and that this principle too often gets lost within workplaces. While pivoting to coding and design in the chaos of 2020, the idea for The Unbranded presented itself as an opportunity to constructively exercise her management skills, and more importantly put her values into practice. She is incredibly grateful and excited to get to do both with such a remarkable team beside her.

HEATHER RANDOLPH

Web Development

Heather is a Web Developer, Visual Designer, and Writer with a passion for storytelling in visual and narrative formats. She has been building websites since 1997 and taking clients on a freelance basis since 2002. While her development expertise lies in HTML and CSS, Heather also works with JavaScript, jQuery, Ruby, and Rails. She creates visual art with Photoshop and Illustrator.
When Heather isn’t writing, developing, or designing, she can be found cooking with her nephews, listening to technology or HP Lovecraft centric podcasts, or scrolling through Netflix trying to decide what to watch.
